strawberry cream Cheesecake

ingredients

  • 200g digestive biscuits
  • 100g melted butter
  • 500g cream cheese
  • 200ml heavy cream
  • sugar
  • 2tsp vanilla extract
  • 200g fresh strawberries
  • 1/2 cup powered sugar

Method of making the strawberry cream cheesecake

  1. preheat the oven to 160c
  2. crush the digestive biscquits into fine crumbd and mix with melted butter
  3. press the biscuit mixture into the base of a springform pan to create a crust
  4. Bake the crust for 8-10 minutes until golden brown. Let it cool while you prepare the filling
  5. Prepare filling: Beat cream cheese, sugar, and vanilla. Add eggs one at a time, then mix in sour cream and heavy cream.
  6. Pour filling into the cooled crust and bake for 50-60 minutes, until set but jiggly. Let cool in the oven for 1 hour, then refrigerate for at least 4 hours.
  7. Prepare topping: In a saucepan, cook strawberries and sugar over medium heat. Mix cornstarch and water, then add to strawberries, stirring until thickened (5-7 minutes). Stir in lemon juice.
  8. Cool the topping to room temperature
  9. Spread the strawberry topping evenly over the chilled cheesecake.
  10. serve and enjoy
